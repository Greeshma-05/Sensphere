Meditation Techniques

Popular Meditation Techniques

Explore these simple techniques to help reduce stress and promote relaxation:

  • Mindfulness Meditation: Focus on your breath and observe your thoughts without judgment.
  • Body Scan Meditation: Gradually bring awareness to each part of your body.
  • Loving-Kindness Meditation: Cultivate compassion and love for yourself and others.
