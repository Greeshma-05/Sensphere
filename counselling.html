How Counsellors Help

Counsellors are trained professionals who provide a safe and supportive environment to discuss your concerns. They use evidence-based techniques to help you manage stress, overcome anxiety, and improve your overall mental well-being. Through sessions, they guide you to identify triggers, develop coping mechanisms, and build resilience.

Tips for Immediate Relief

Follow these steps to reduce stress quickly:

  1. Take deep breaths for at least 2 minutes.
  2. Focus on a calming image or thought.
  3. Engage in a simple activity like drawing or listening to music.
